What happens in a volcanic eruption?

A volcano acts as a vent for the Earth which is where eruptions occur. They look like a mountain but with a hole on top. Volcanoes can spew lava, dangerous gases, ash and rock which can be life-threatening if you live near the proximity of a volcano. While the lava flow may be as dangeroud because you can still get away from it, the gases and the ash can be lethal if you breath near it. There can also be after effects like an earthquakes, landslides, and tsunamis if the volcano is near water. The hot ashes coming out of the volcano can also cause wildfires.
